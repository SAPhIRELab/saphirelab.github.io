Master's and Ph.D Students

I have indicated various skills that I am looking for when hiring students, which include both necessary requirements. If you think your interests match with mine and you meet the requirements below, please fill out the form. Given the large volume of emails I receive, please do not send an email. Filling out the form is sufficient and I will check it regularly.

  1. In case of a potential match:
    • I will email you to schedule a ~45-minute call. Please identify an available slot in my calendar.
    • I may ask you to:
      • Read selected papers and identify interesting research problems you would like to work on; and/or
      • Complete a simple programming task (no algorithms) on platform like CodeSignal.
  2. If you do not hear from me within one month:
    • Please assume you were not selected.
    • Do not take this personally â€” this is a competitive process with many applicants from diverse backgrounds worldwide.
    • Not being selected is not a reflection of your ability or potential.

Necessary Requirements

  1. Programming Expertise
    • Strong foundation in programming and debugging; and confident with implementation and experimentation
    • Comfortable replicating, executing, and refactoring existing code.
    • Strong command of Python (required): Experience and comfort with ML libraries such as PyTorch (primary framework), vLLM, HuggingFace, etc.
  2. Background in Machine Learning
    • Solid understanding of machine learning, deep learning, and generative models.
    • Knowledge equivalent to undergraduate coursework (both theoretical and practical).
    • Example relevant courses:
      • Deep Learning course by Ali Ghodsi
      • LLM courses from NPTEL (IIT Delhi, IIT Madras)
      • Stanford course on "Transformers and LLMs"
  3. Clear Communication
    • Ability to clearly describe problems and articulate potential solutions.
    • Comfort discussing academic papers and identifying their limitations.
    • Can propose tentative solutions for hurles in implementations.
  4. Critical Thinking
    • Systematically review existing literature.
    • Critically assess limitations and gaps in prior work.
    • Design targeted next steps and experiments to test hypotheses and demonstrate those limitations.
I expect students to demonstrate genuine eagerness and enthusiasm to quickly learn and apply relevant concepts to research problems. The ability to think critically and approach challenges with curiosity and passion is key.

Undergraduates

If you are interested in exploring research, please apply by filling out the form.
  • You will likely work closely with a Master's or Ph.D. student on a specific project supervised by me.
  • Strong grades in fundamental courses such as programming and machine learning are expected.
  • The "necessary requirements" listed above apply.

Postdocs

Please email me directly with your CV and research statement (max. 2 pages) with contributions and future directions. Generally, I expect postdoctoral applicants to have at least one paper in reputed venue.

Collaborations (Faculty / PIs / Industry Research Scientists)

If we share common research interests, I welcome joint projects with faculty, PIs, and industry researchers. Please email me to discuss potential collaborations, including joint work with your students or mine. Collaboration depends on current workload and resource availability, but we can revisit opportunities as availability permits.
