Undergraduates, Master's and Ph.D Students

If you think your research interests align and you meet the necessary requirements below, please fill out this form. Due to large volume of emails, I prefer filling out the form over receiving an email (to avoid missing some mails).
  • In case of a potential match, I will email you to schedule a ~45-minute call during an available slot in my calendar. I might also ask you to review papers to identify limitations and future directions; or other ways to assess compatibility.
  • For undergraduates, I am looking for good grades in programming, information security, and machine learning courses.

What am I looking for?

  1. Excellent Proficiency with Programming and Debugging
    • Strong proficiency in implementing ML algorithms with PyTorch, vLLM, HuggingFace, etc.
    • Comfortable with rapid prototyping, replicating and refactoring other implementations.
      • Demonstrated through programming competitions, hackathons, CTFs, etc.
  2. Strong understanding of ML theory/algorithms/architectures (+ generative models)
    • Example courses with content that students should know:
      • Deep learning course by Ali Ghodsi
      • NPTEL LLM courses by IIT Delhi or IIT Madras
      • Transformers and LLMs course by Stanford
  3. Critical Thinking: Can identify limitations of prior work and think of future directions
    • Prior research experience demonstrated via projects, publications, or internships.
Overall, I expect students to demonstrate enthusiasm to quickly learn and apply relevant concepts to research problems.

Postdocs

Please email me directly with your CV and research statement (max. 2 pages) with contributions and future directions. Generally, I expect postdoctoral applicants to have at least one paper in reputed venue.
