I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Waterloo advised by N. Asokan. I am part of the Cryptography, Security, and Privacy (CrySP) group and the Cybersecurity and Privacy Institute (CPI).

Research: I work on enhancing trust in machine learning systems by

  • Assessing Risks: Define realistic threat models to evaluate novel risks (to security, privacy, fairness, transparency)
  • Designing Defenses: Formulate requirements (e.g., effective, efficient, robust) to design practical defenses
  • Holistic System Analysis: Study practical deployment concerns (e.g., interactions among defenses and risks)
  • Technical Governance: Design technical mechanisms for accountability and regulatory compliance

Selected Awards (full list):

  • IBM PhD Fellowship, 2024 News
  • Distinguished Paper Award @ IEEE S&P, 2024 News

Past: I completed my masters from University of Waterloo (advised by N. Asokan), and my undergraduate from IIIT-Delhi. During my undergraduate, I worked with Antoine Boutet (INRIA Privatics) and Reza Shokri (NUS, Singapore).
